Products

Products

We provide the following products:

Zebra Blinds

Zebra blinds, sometimes called Transition or Vision blinds. For spaces like living rooms, bedrooms, and offices, zebra blinds are the ideal choice. With sturdy, long-lasting textiles and components, they are also simple to operate, clean, and maintain.

Roller Blinds

The variety of fabrics, colours, patterns, and options available for Roller Blinds allows you to choose the ideal style for every area in your home. Additionally, Roller Blinds are very affordable, quick and simple to install, and easy to clean so you can keep them looking brand new.

Shangri-La blinds

Shangri la blinds are made of two layers: a polyester material that is perforated. An exquisite darkening cloth that allows sunlight to pass through while being hung between two transparent layers. With Shangri-La Sheer Blinds' beautiful designs, you can balance light and shadow.

Vertical Blinds

Homeowners continue to prefer vertical blinds as their window covering of choice. They provide a wonderful balance of design and use. Additionally, vertical blinds are inexpensive and simple to maintain.

Motorized Blinds

Who want's to roll up and down each time when you can just click a button to control the blinds. You can use your phone or remote to roll the blinds up and down. You can even set a timer for any action.

Cordless Blinds

Perfect for the clients who have kids at home. You can use the 2 buttons on the rod to pull up and down. We always provide warranty for any damages except the forceful breaking of rod.
